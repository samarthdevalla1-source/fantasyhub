Winner — Fantasy Intelligence
Winner is a data-driven fantasy football web app that helps managers make smarter roster decisions using real NFL stats, AI-powered recommendations, and advanced player analytics. Built with React, Supabase, and the Claude API, it pulls live player data from the Sleeper API and runs a full PPR scoring engine to give you accurate projections, trend analysis, and personalized start/sit advice.
Features

Roster Management — Build your roster with real NFL players, auto-generate your optimal lineup, and swap players between starters and bench
Player Database — Browse 700+ active NFL players with real 2025 season stats, search and filter by position, and sort by projected or average points
Player Profiles — Click any player to see their headshot, weekly points chart, season summary, and statistically similar players
Data Science — Breakout Detector flags hot and cold players, a Similarity Engine finds comparable players using a distance formula, and a Roster Grade scores your team across star power, positional balance, injury risk, and bench depth
AI Analyst — Powered by Claude, gives personalized start/sit recommendations and answers any fantasy question based on your actual roster
